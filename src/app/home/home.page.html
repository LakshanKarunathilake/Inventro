Authorities

Assets manager is the main authorized person of the system. Only assets manager, CEO, Event organizers, Department heads can book the assets. Any registered employee of the company can request any asset through the system. Only high management have the authority to generate reports.

Rules and Regulations

Employees should return the assets maximum in two weeks. Employees should take responsibility for the assets when it is under his/her responsibility. Any employee can report breakdowns to the authority.
